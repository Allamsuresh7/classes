Kalki 2898 AD first glimpse

The first glimpse of Kalki 2898 AD was unveiled at the 2023 San Diego Comic Con (SDCC). “When the world is taken over by darkness, a force will rise,” reads the video as we see armed soldiers storming a fort. Deepika Padukone appears in short hair in a file of female recruits.
The teaser oozes dystopia and Indofuturism as Prabhas makes his entry in a cool exosuit. “What is Project K?,” he is asked, while catching a deadly lance with his hand.


The title of Nag Ashwin’s Project K has been officially revealed as Kalki 2898 AD. Starring a massive Pan-Indian cast of Prabhas, Deepika Padukone, Amitabh Bachchan, Kamal Haasan and Disha Patani and Saswata Chatterjee, the film is an epic, mythology-inspired sci-fi spectacular set in the future
